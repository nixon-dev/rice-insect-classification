How to train an Insect Identification Model

  • 1. Organize your data:
    • Create a folder named "dataset".
    • Inside "dataset", create two subfolders: "train" and "val". These will hold your training and validation data.
    • Within "train" and "val", create folders for each insect class you want to identify. Make sure the folder names are identical in both "train" and "val".
    • Place your insect images inside their corresponding class folders. For example, if you have images of butterflies and bees, you would have folders named "butterflies" and "bees" inside both "train" and "val".
  • 2. Prepare your data
    • Compress the "dataset" folder into a zip file. This step might be required depending on the platform you use to train your model.
  • 3. Train your model:
    • Open Google Collab notebook.
    • Upload your "dataset.zip" file (if you compressed it) to the Colab notebook environment.
    • Follow the instructions in the Colab notebook to train your insect identification model using your prepared dataset.
  • 4. Upload your model
    • Open Model Setting to upload your "best.pt" you downloaded from the Google Colab.
    • And you're done!
